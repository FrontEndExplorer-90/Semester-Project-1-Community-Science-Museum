Get Involved

Support Us

Your support keeps the Community Science Museum thriving! Donations help us create engaging exhibits, host inspiring events, and keep science accessible to everyone.You can also contribute by donating unique items or collections to enrich our exhibits.


If you have something special to share, our Collections Department would love to hear from you. Every contribution makes a difference. Join us in inspiring curiosity and fostering discovery for the entire community. Thank you for being part of our mission!

Volunteer With Us

Be part of the Community Science Museum team by volunteering your time and skills! Volunteers help maintain exhibits, support events, and create a welcoming space for visitors. As a volunteer, you’ll enjoy exclusive perks like behind-the-scenes access, staff-only lectures, and monthly gatherings to share ideas and collaborate on future projects.


Whether it’s assisting with exhibits or contributing your unique talents, there’s a place for you. Reach out today to learn how you can get involved!

Internship Opportunities

Are you passionate about science and eager to work in a museum? An internship at the Community Science Museum could be the perfect opportunity for you! Gain hands-on experience while learning from dedicated professionals and academics who are enthusiastic about sharing the wonders of nature and discovery.


Whether you’re exploring exhibits or engaging with visitors, this is your chance to grow and inspire others. Start your journey with us—contact our team to learn more!
