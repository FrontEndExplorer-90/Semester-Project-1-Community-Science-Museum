Explore Our Exhibits

Discover the wonders of science through our multi-level museum! Use the floor maps below to explore each section and learn more about what awaits you.

Ground Floor

Start your journey on the ground floor, home to the Café, Planetarium, Cosmology Exhibit, and Indoor Playground!

Café: Enjoy fresh snacks, coffee, and meals in a cozy setting.

Planetarium: Explore the wonders of the universe in our immersive dome theater.

Cosmology Exhibit: Discover constellations, galaxies, and the history of space exploration.

Indoor Playground: A safe and fun space for kids to play while parents take a break.

First Floor

Explore the first floor, featuring the Ecology, Evolution, and Biology exhibits, as well as the Lecture Hall and the meeting point for Night at the Museum!

Ecology Exhibit: Dive into the challenges and solutions for creating a sustainable future.

Evolution Exhibit: Trace the origins of life and how species have evolved over time.

Lecture Hall: Attend fascinating lectures, like those by Professor Sheila Widnall!

Biology Exhibit: Explore the microscopic world and medical breakthroughs like never before.

Night at the Museum Meetup Point: Your adventure starts here with exciting nighttime discoveries!

Second Floor

Discover the wonders of the second floor, featuring the Medicine Exhibit, Robotics and AI, Energetica, the Insects Exhibit, and the Botanical Garden!

Medicine Exhibit: Learn about groundbreaking advancements in medicine, from ancient practices to cutting-edge biotechnology.

Robotics and AI: Experience the future of science with hands-on robotics and artificial intelligence demonstrations.

Energetica Exhibit: Explore the fascinating world of energy, its history, and its role in powering the future.

Insects Exhibit: Dive into the mesmerizing world of insects and discover their crucial role in our ecosystem.

Botanical Garden: Stroll through lush greenery and experience the biodiversity of plant life from around the world.
