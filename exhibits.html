Explore Our Exhibits

Discover the wonders of science through our multi-level museum! Use the floor maps below to explore each section and learn more about what awaits you.

Ground Floor

Start your journey on the ground floor, home to the Café, Planetarium, Cosmology Exhibit, and Indoor Playground!

Café: Enjoy fresh snacks, coffee, and meals in a cozy setting.

Planetarium: Explore the wonders of the universe in our immersive dome theater.

Cosmology Exhibit: Discover constellations, galaxies, and the history of space exploration.

Indoor Playground: A safe and fun space for kids to play while parents take a break.

First Floor

The first floor features the Planetarium, special exhibit spaces, and areas for interactive learning.

Second Floor

Visit the second floor for the Robotics and AI Exhibit, the Botanical Gardens, and the Insects Exhibition.
