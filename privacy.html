Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2024

Welcome to the Community Science Museum! Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or interact with our services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Such as your name, email address, and phone number when you fill out forms (e.g., contact forms, event registrations).
  • Non-Personal Information: Such as your browser type, operating system, and browsing behavior (e.g., pages visited, time spent on our site).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Improve your experience on our website.
  • Respond to your inquiries or requests.
  • Send updates about museum events, exhibits, or programs (only if you opt-in to receive communications).

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law or to fulfill the services you request.

3. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to:

  • Enhance your browsing experience by remembering your preferences.
  • Analyze website performance and visitor behavior through tools like Google Analytics.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may impact your ability to access certain features of our website.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Request that we delete your personal information, subject to legal obligations.

To exercise your rights, please contact us at privacy@communitysciencemuseum.com.

6. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
