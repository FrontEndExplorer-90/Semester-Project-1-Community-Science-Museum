Prices And Tours:

"Explore the museum like never before!"

Join our exciting guided tours every 60 minutes to dive deeper into the coolest parts of the museum! Whether you’re a curious kid, a teen explorer, or a parent ready to learn something new, our tours are packed with fun facts and discoveries.

How to Book:

Reserve your spot online or at the museum’s front desk for just 70kr per person. Don’t miss out on this adventure!

School-class, daycare or group of 6+? Please contact us for more information on our guided tours!

Accessibility:

"Everyone’s welcome to explore the wonders of science!"

Our museum is fully accessible, with ramps for wheelchairs and stroller-friendly pathways. For our visually impaired visitors, we offer audio guides and braille display signs to make sure no discovery is out of reach. Because science is for everyone!

Refreshments?

"Fuel up for more fun at our cozy café!"

Take a break from your adventure in the World of Science at our café. Enjoy tasty snacks, refreshing drinks, and kid-friendly meals perfect for recharging before your next discovery! Parents, there's great coffee too!

Souvenirs?

"Take a piece of the museum home with you!"

Our souvenir shop is packed with cool science-themed gifts, fun activity kits, interactive toys, and keepsakes that will remind you of your day of discovery. Whether you're looking for a gadget, a game, or just a little something special, there's something for everyone!
