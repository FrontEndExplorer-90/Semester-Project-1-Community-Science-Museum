Prices And Tours:

Free Entrance for everyone!

Guided tour every 60 minutes in different parts of the museum. Tours can be booked online or directly in the museum and cost 70kr per person.

School-class, daycare or group of 6+? Please contact us for more information on our guided tours!

Accessibility:

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps. It also has audio guides and braille display signs for the visually impaired.

Refreshments?

Our museum's café has everything you need for a break while exploring the World of Science!

Souvenirs?

Our souvenir-shop has a wide range of gifts, keepsakes, toys, activity boxes, and much more.
