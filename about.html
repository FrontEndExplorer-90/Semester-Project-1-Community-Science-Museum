About Our Museum

“Explore together”

Step into a world where science isn’t just learned—it’s experienced! At the Community Science Museum, we’re on a mission to make science exciting, interactive, and accessible to everyone, no matter your age. From the first sparks of fire to unraveling the mysteries of distant galaxies, science has shaped the story of human curiosity and discovery. Now, it’s your turn to dive into that story! Our museum is a space where you can uncover the secrets of the universe, explore the marvels of life, and maybe even spark a passion for asking the “whys” and “hows” of the world around you. With over 1,000 exciting exhibits, there’s something here for everyone—from budding inventors to space explorers in the making! Many exhibits are hands-on, inviting you to experiment, play, and watch science come to life right before your eyes (though we’ll admit, the dinosaurs tend to stay still… until the museum closes for the night). So, bring your curiosity, and let’s embark on an unforgettable journey through the fascinating world of science together. Who knows? You might just leave inspired to be the next great inventor, scientist, or explorer!

How It All Began

The Community Science Museum opened its doors in 2002 with a vision to make science accessible to everyone. Starting with just 200 exhibits, it has grown into a vibrant hub of discovery, hosting over 1,000 exhibits and welcoming millions of visitors from around the world.

Why Visit Us?

  • Discover over 1,000 interactive exhibits.
  • Experience exciting events like "Night at the Museum."
  • Learn about cutting-edge science in our workshops.
  • Get hands-on with robotics, biology, and more.
