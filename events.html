Coming Soon to the Community Science Museum!

Get ready to experience the magic of nature’s power! The Energetica exhibition, on loan from the famous NEMO Science Museum in Amsterdam, is heading our way!With hands-on installations, you’ll feel the energy of the wind, sun, and water—and discover how we use these forces to shape our world. Don’t miss this incredible adventure!
